About Me

Hey, I'm someone who loves building stuff—especially when it's related to robotics. I’ve always been curious about how things work, and that naturally pulled me into creating projects where I get to design, build, and sometimes even troubleshoot for hours (which I weirdly enjoy). Robotics gives me a mix of hardware and software to play around with, and I like the challenge that comes with it.

Apart from that, I have a growing inpngprofile2.pngest in coding. I know HTML, CSS, and Java, and I’ve used them in different projects, mostly for web stuff. I enjoy making things look good and work smoothly. Whether it’s styling with CSS or writing logic in Java, I’m always down to experiment and learn something new.

I'm always looking for ways to improve, try out new ideas, and push myself a little more with every project. For me, learning by doing is the best part. This portfolio is just a small part of what I’ve worked on—and there’s a lot more I’m planning to build.

My Projects

Human-Following Robo Car

Built a robo car using Arduino that can detect and follow a person using ultrasonic sensors. I handled the wiring, sensor setup, and coding to control the motors based on distance. It was a fun hands-on project combining both hardware and software.

Personal Portfolio Website

Designed and built my own portfolio using HTML and CSS with a dark theme and interactive images. This project helped me improve my front-end skills and focus on layout, styling, and smooth user experience—without using JavaScript.

Solar Power Bank for Mobile Charging

Designed and built a portable solar power bank that can charge mobile devices using solar energy. I worked on connecting solar panels with a battery and regulating the output to safely charge phones. This project taught me a lot about renewable energy, circuit design, and real-world problem-solving.
